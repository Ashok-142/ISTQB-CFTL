## 📋 14 Business Outcomes

A certified tester can…

1. Understand what testing is and why it is beneficial
2. Understand fundamental concepts of software testing
3. Identify the test approach and activities depending on context
4. Assess and improve the quality of documentation
5. Increase effectiveness and efficiency of testing
6. Align the test process with the SDLC
7. Understand test management principles
8. Write and communicate clear defect reports
9. Understand factors influencing testing priorities and effort
10. Work as part of a cross-functional team
11. Know risks and benefits of test automation
12. Identify essential skills required for testing
13. Understand the impact of risk on testing
14. Effectively report on test progress and quality
